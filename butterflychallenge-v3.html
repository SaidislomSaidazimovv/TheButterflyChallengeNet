Why this exists

The world built systems for almost everything. Not for mental health.

Mental health touches every family, every school, every team, every workplace, every community on earth.

The person sitting across from you at dinner. The one who has been quieter than usual. The one you keep meaning to check in on. The one you almost lost.

You don't always know. And by the time you do — it has sometimes been too long.

The Butterfly Challenge exists because of that silence. Not to talk about it. To break it.

The Butterfly Sign

Two hands.
One signal.

The Butterfly Sign is how you show up for mental health when you do not have the words. The first universal gesture for care since the peace sign — made by anyone, in any language, in any room on earth.

No diagnosis. No perfect moment. No language barrier. No words required. Just two hands and the willingness to show up.
